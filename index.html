About PPMHSS

Founded in 1976 by the Nediyiruppu Muslim Educational Association to uplift educationally backward communities, PPMHSS has grown into a leading institution in Malappuram with strong academics, engaging student life, and modern infrastructure.

Students

~4,000 currently enrolled (8–12)

Staff

~100–150 faculty members

Divisions

Over 55–70 class divisions

HS Started

2000–01 (Science & Humanities)

New Streams

Added CS, Sociology & Commerce in 2002

Achievements

  • 9‐year run of district trophies for highest pass %
  • "Dr. Mohiyidheen" state-level Arabic excellence award
  • Science fair wins at state & national levels (Shimoga, Salem, Ahmedabad, Hyderabad, Chennai)

Key Programs & Activities

PPMHSS offers a vibrant mix: hobby clubs, debates, creative writing, exhibitions, youth parliament, Scouts/Guides, NCC, sports, music, dance, workshops, career counseling, annual events and more.
