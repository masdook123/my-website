About Our School

PPMHSS (Punnathala Public Muslim Higher Secondary School) is dedicated to providing quality education and holistic development for students in the Malappuram district of Kerala. Our school combines academic excellence with strong moral values.

Our History

Established in 1957, PPMHSS has been a pioneer in shaping the future of thousands of students. Over the years, we have expanded our academic programs, modernized our infrastructure, and embraced technology to enhance learning experiences.
